About LearnSync

LearnSync LMS

LearnSync is the official Learning Management System of Governor Mariano E. Villafuerte Community College. Designed to modernize education, it connects students and instructors in a unified digital environment, making learning accessible, organized, and engaging.

Features

  • Class Management & Materials Distribution
  • Assignment Submission & Grading
  • AI-Powered Learning Assistant
  • Deadline Tracking & Notifications
